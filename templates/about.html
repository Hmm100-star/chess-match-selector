About This Tool

Chess Match Selector streamlines pairing students, collecting round results, and rolling those stats back into your master sheet.

Purpose: provide a complete, repeatable loop for running scholastic chess clubsâ€”generate balanced pairings, capture wins/losses and homework accuracy, then update each student's history automatically.

How to use it

  1. Visit the Pairings page, upload your Student_Information.csv, and adjust the win/homework weights if needed.
  2. Download the enriched next_matches.csv, run the round, and fill in the result/homework columns for both colours (bye rows included).
  3. Head to the Update Sheet page, upload the latest Student_Information.csv plus the completed match sheet, and download the refreshed master file.

Keep iterating: each updated master sheet feeds right back into the pairing page so your students' ratings reflect every round.
