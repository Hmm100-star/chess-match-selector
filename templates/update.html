How to fill the match CSV

  • Use the Who Won column with W (White), B (Black), T (Tie), or leave blank if no result is ready.
  • Enter homework correct/incorrect counts for each player. Leave blank for zero.
  • Bye rows still count toward color history. Use W in Who Won if you treat a bye as a win.
